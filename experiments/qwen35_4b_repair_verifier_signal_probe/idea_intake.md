# Idea Intake: Repair-Verifier Signal Probe

## Trigger

The dose-scale null closed the last SFT route to menders: fresh-instance
transfer sat exactly at the untrained controls' guess floor after an
order-of-magnitude dose increase. The one remaining mechanism class is
on-policy episode training — and per calibrate-and-diverge, its GATE gets
tested before its treatment is funded. On-policy learning at this skill
has signal only if the model can VERIFY eliminative repairs it cannot
propose.

## Mechanism precedent

C29: after generation collapsed, the model remained a read-only verifier
(2AFC 0.81) — proposal and verification dissociate in this model. C47:
no-think judges are substrate-scoped and the think-judge rescues. C44:
always give the 4B chain-of-thought. If that dissociation extends to
two-round eliminative repair, an on-policy loop has a selection signal
to climb; if verification is also at chance, the loop has nothing to
climb and the class closes without spending its charter.

## Question

Presented with both evidence rounds and two candidate fixes — the unique
survivor of both rounds versus a distractor consistent with round one
alone — does the hygiene_explore composite pick the survivor above
chance (frozen bar: think-arm accuracy ≥ 0.65 with the 95% CI excluding
0.5)?

## Calibration

Cheap (one eval event, 400 judgments, no training, no benchmark seed)
and decisive in both directions: SIGNAL_PRESENT funds the on-policy
intake with a mechanism argument; SIGNAL_ABSENT closes the final class
and completes the program map at demonstrated-not-confirmed. Prior on
signal: genuinely uncertain — C29's dissociation supports it (~0.81
there), but the eliminative two-round structure is exactly what C38/C48
say the model cannot induce; verification may reduce to execution
(checking a fix against both trials is execute-mode, which the model HAS)
— that reading favors signal. ~50–60% on SIGNAL_PRESENT.
