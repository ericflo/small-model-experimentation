# Idea Intake: Menders Dose-Scale

## Trigger

The confirmation localized the program goal to exactly one family: nine
hold against base on every sealed seed, two full 10/10 sweeps exist
across four seeds, and menders alone gates the rest — 0-margin ties on
every failing seed. Every small-dose pedagogy at the menders-shaped skill
is closed by kill rule (asserted repair, demonstrated search, 80-row
episode feedback) and the thinking-budget lever is closed by two
preregistered stops. The one mechanism class the kill rules permit is
DOSE SCALE, with C43 as precedent: partial installs were data-limited
(induction 0.087→0.40 with more data), and every failed menders attempt
was 80–160 rows.

## Question

At 10× the failed dose — 800 episode-feedback rows across eight invented
legality-bounded formalisms — does fresh-instance transfer move at all
from the 80-row cell's 0/20, and if the dose promotes under the
calibrated gates, does menders move at medium?

## Honest calibration

The 80-row dose installed NOTHING in-domain (0/20, below untrained
controls), so scale must overcome a zero rather than amplify a signal —
the C43 precedent amplified a 0.087. Prior on fresh-instance transfer
appearing at 10×: ~25–35%. Prior on the full chain (transfer AND
promotion AND menders > 0 at medium): ~10–20%. The preregistered
dose-response reading is decisive either way: any nonzero transfer at
10× reopens the family with a measured dose curve; a zero at 10× closes
the scale class for this skill honestly, leaving the zero-root rebuild
and genuinely new mechanism classes (on-policy episode training) as the
remaining intakes.
