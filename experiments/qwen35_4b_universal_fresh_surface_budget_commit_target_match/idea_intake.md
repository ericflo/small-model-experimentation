## Novelty Claim

First universal-line trial to (a) test whether the designed-dose effect is surface-general by re-dosing the proven per-skill distribution on entirely fresh surfaces, and (b) test a designed bounded-computation lesson — resource-bounded ordered scan with a hard check allowance, immediate stop on first hit, and a mandatory BUDGET commit on exhaustion, with a planted decoy immediately past the cutoff so violating the allowance is parseable-but-wrong — as a substitution ablation at exactly matched dose, rows, updates, and three-axis loss exposure.

## Mechanism

The designed dose installs generic procedure-following circuits; its residual failure mode at deployment is termination (cap contacts and unparsed answers), which the budget lesson attacks as content — teaching monitored, contract-bounded computation that commits canonically — rather than as a loss-weight knob (falsified by close_weight) or an idealized trace surface (falsified by state_table/scaffold). The explanation is false for arm D if it fails the strict local wins over parent and matched replay; the budget increment is false if arm B does not beat arm D's deployment behavior at identical exposure.
