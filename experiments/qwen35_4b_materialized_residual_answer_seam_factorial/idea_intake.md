# Idea intake: materialized residual answer-seam factorial

## Rough idea

Before interpreting another materialized-residual generation run, select a
deployable short answer interface on known-answer echo tasks. Only an interface
that reliably emits the actually scored program event may open disjoint
mechanics tasks.

## Closest near-duplicates

- `qwen35_4b_materialized_residual_sibling_search_fresh_replication`: durable
  parent; generation ABI invalid and cheap viability ranker cleanly negative.
- `qwen35_4b_commit_slot_semantic_power_replication`: a fixed `First:` slot
  exposes semantic choice, but its J-value follow-up fails and the slot is not a
  two-operation program emitter.
- `qwen35_4b_forced_commit_jacobian_value_transport`: close-only forced commit
  does not create answer mode.

## Why this is distinct

This is an interface-selection firewall, not a rerun of residual mechanics and
not a new J-space coordinate. The initial three-policy sketch was superseded by
the adversarially required full 2x2 crossing think/no-think with
freeform/literal-`PROGRAM:` emission on separate known-answer calibration
tasks. It freezes a winner without mechanics outcomes and makes every mechanism
arm ineligible if the ABI fails.

## Expected decision

Either one interface clears >=90% exact echo/parse and <=5% cap contact, making
a clean disjoint residual test possible, or the whole generative branch stops
without another ambiguous mechanism null.

## Forbidden shortcuts

- no larger cap as the sole change;
- no parser relaxation after seeing output;
- no teacher-forced answer likelihood as a substitute for emitted programs;
- no reuse of parent task/request IDs or stage seeds;
- no reopening the failed cheap viability/top-four ranker;
- no benchmark, qualification, confirmation, or hidden-outcome peeking.
