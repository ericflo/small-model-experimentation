# Preregistration

Frozen before any task-model output for this experiment.

## Primary hypothesis

A split-branch, verifier-backed continuation-value rule can identify locally
useful states for both same-origin C54 teachers on the strongest existing
single-checkpoint student. Conditional on that prerequisite, positive-only
same-prefix MOPD can produce one Qwen3.5-4B checkpoint whose held-out joint
capability is above both teachers, the initial soup, visible routing, matched
controls, and execution-filtered sample-more without retention loss.

## Fixed policies

- `quick`: the independently regenerated `quick_blend` merged composite from
  `qwen35_4b_pareto_policy_integration`.
- `deep`: the independently regenerated `deep_apex` merged composite from that
  experiment.
- `soup`: an independently regenerated convex LoRA-delta merge with weights
  0.40 quick and 0.60 deep. It is both the initial student and frozen retention
  teacher.
- `student_r`: the explicitly merged output of round `r`; all on-policy states
  in round `r+1` must be generated by this exact checkpoint.
- No model other than the pinned `Qwen/Qwen3.5-4B` revision may generate,
  score, teach, judge, or provide a capability source.

The source adapter/config/model hashes, regenerated soup hash, composite merge
receipts, and behavior-change canary are mandatory provenance, not performance
evidence.

## State construction

Only the copied procedural gym may produce training or gate states. The 12
trained families are eligible for updates. `brinework` and `spindle` are
transfer-only and never enter routing or training.

Each qualification block first runs the soup stochastically on a fixed fresh
candidate set.

- Atom eligibility: verified terminal score is below `0.999999`. The exact
  student thought prefix contains
  `min(T-1, clamp(floor(0.5 * T), 8, 128))` of the student's retained thinking
  tokens, where `T >= 2` is the retained thought length; it never includes an
  injected close token. The prompt token IDs plus this prefix are the state.
- Episode eligibility: terminal score is below `0.999999`. Replay the exact
  student-visible history and select the state immediately before its first
  `action_ok=false` action; if every action is syntactically valid, select the
  state before its final action. The environment is reconstructed only from
  family, public procedural seed, and the replayed visible actions. Hidden
  simulator state is never rendered.
- Eligible states are selected round-robin over predeclared
  `(family, kind, level)` cells before any teacher branch exists. Each block
  requires 144 atoms and 48 episode states. Insufficient eligible states is a
  design-feasibility stop, not permission to reuse seeds or alter the mix.

The two qualification blocks and all training rounds use disjoint seed
namespaces. A prompt, state, or generated item may not appear in more than one
block or round.

## Split-branch route estimand

For each exact state `s` and each policy `p` in `{quick, deep, student}`, draw
four selection continuations and four disjoint audit continuations using the
same decode settings and inference backend. Let `V_sel(p,s)` and
`V_audit(p,s)` be the corresponding mean verified terminal scores after `p`
takes over from `s`.

The frozen routing function is:

1. choose `quick` iff `V_sel(quick,s)` is strictly greater than both
   `V_sel(deep,s)` and `V_sel(student,s)`;
2. choose `deep` under the symmetric condition;
3. abstain on every tie or otherwise.

There is no positive margin beyond strict inequality. In particular, an
advantage of any size can route. Selection outcomes choose the teacher and are
never reused to estimate its effect.

For a selected teacher `t(s)` and alternate `a(s)`, the two primary audit
contrasts are:

- `D_student(s) = V_audit(t(s),s) - V_audit(student,s)`;
- `D_alternate(s) = V_audit(t(s),s) - V_audit(a(s),s)`.

All means and uncertainty are computed at the state level. Bootstrap resamples
within frozen `(family, kind, level)` cells and then macro-averages cells; it
never treats the eight continuations as eight independent states.

## Route qualification

MOPD is authorized only if all conditions hold:

1. both quick and deep route at least 16 states in each block;
2. for each selected teacher separately, both `D_student` and `D_alternate`
   have positive means in each block;
3. for each selected teacher and each contrast, the pooled one-sided 95%
   stratified-bootstrap lower bound is above zero; and
4. the combined routed policy satisfies the same block-sign and pooled-bound
   conditions against both student and alternate.

There is no teacher capability effect-size floor. Minimum support is an
identifiability/data-supply requirement, not a performance threshold. Failure
of any condition writes a terminal receipt and stops before teacher logits,
locality, training, controls, confirmation, or benchmarks.

## Conditional on-policy update

Four rounds are fixed. Each round samples fresh states from the exact current
student, applies the identical four-branch selection rule (no audit branches),
and gathers consume-once units. States that no longer have positive estimated
teacher advantage abstain. The unit mixture is:

- 75% capability units, split equally between quick- and deep-routed states;
- 25% anchor units, with the frozen soup scoring exact current-student
  trajectories on non-routed or successful states.

The selected frozen policy scores the current student's exact completion at
every prefix. Training minimizes the corrected teacher-top-50 reverse-KL term

`p_s log(p_s / p_t) - p_s + p_t`

using full-vocabulary normalizers and teacher top-50 indices. Injected
force-close tokens are masked. A state contributes at most its final 256
natural policy positions once per round. No teacher-generated continuation is
substituted into the primary loss.

Round loss, full-gradient finiteness, top-50 teacher/student overlap, entropy,
sampled/input token counts, and exact checkpoint hashes are recorded. Any
non-finite value, mean corrected top-k loss above 0.10, missing equal-teacher
quota, replayed state, or decreasing mean overlap stops training and preserves
the unsafe checkpoint receipt.

## Locality gate

Before a full round, perform five implementation-identical primary updates and
explicitly merge the adapter. On a frozen batch-of-1 exact-logit probe, require:

- mean per-row median centered non-target logit drift `<= 0.10`;
- relative full-vocabulary entropy drop `<= 0.10`;
- mean corrected top-k loss `<= 0.10`;
- finite logits/gradients and no collapse/closure guard failure.

Failure stops before all full training. This threshold is a safety/locality
constraint inherited from C52, not a capability effect-size hurdle.

## Matched controls

All trainable controls use the exact primary state identities, number of
optimizer updates, target positions, current-student rollouts, and initial
corrected-loss pressure wherever their definition permits.

- `shuffled_route`: permute the selected quick/deep identities within teacher
  and state-kind quotas, preserving teacher counts but breaking advantage.
- `coarse_route`: assign quick to L1-L2 atoms and deep to L3-L6 atoms plus all
  episodes—the prior visible label—on the primary state pool.
- `fixed_deep`: use the previously dominant deep teacher on every primary
  capability state.
- `offpolicy_sft`: train on the highest-scoring selection continuation from the
  chosen teacher rather than dense logits on the student's trajectory.
- `soup25/50/75`: independently construct explicit convex parameter baselines.
- `soup`: no-update strongest one-checkpoint incumbent.

The primary is trained at seeds 42, 43, and 44 with no checkpoint selection.
Controls use their frozen single seeds and are not tuned after observation.

## Final procedural decision

Two fresh greedy confirmation blocks cover all 14 families, atoms L1-L6, and
episodes L2/L3/L5. The deployable visible router uses quick for L1-L2 atoms and
deep otherwise. The sample-more arm draws eight soup continuations and returns
the verifier-best completion under the same runner; all eight samples count in
the token ledger.

The seed-42 final checkpoint is the primary artifact. It passes only if:

1. its equal-weight quick/deep joint paired delta has a one-sided 95% lower
   bound above zero versus quick, deep, soup, visible routing, and every
   trainable/parameter control;
2. both independent block means are positive for every primary comparison;
3. in each block, its quick and deep stratum means each exceed the better of
   the two source-policy means in that stratum;
4. seed 43 and seed 44 have positive joint deltas versus soup and both source
   policies in both blocks;
5. no frozen retention-anchor cell regresses by more than 0.02 and neither
   transfer-family macro regresses by more than 0.02 versus soup; and
6. seed 42 greedy joint performance is above execution-filtered soup
   best-of-8, with sampled-token cost reported.

No fixed gain magnitude is required. If these conditions fail after teachers
qualified, the result is a negative for this advantage-routed integration
recipe, not permission to choose another seed/checkpoint or weaken a gate.

## Benchmark firewall

Only a passing machine-readable procedural receipt can enable the already
documented run-only benchmark CLI. No experiment Python may import or name
benchmark family internals. No benchmark file, item, transcript, or item-level
result may be read or used for training. Every registered event must be
reported without exclusion. The C54 large-n correction landed before this
design lock and is binding: run three paired quick events and at least eight
paired medium events from the frozen namespace because medium n=3 arm means
have about ±0.03 noise and are provisional. A benchmark pass additionally
requires positive paired aggregate delta versus quick, deep, soup, visible
routing, and matched sample-more on both registered tiers.

## Interpretation map

- Route gate fails: the source pair lacks reproducible local complementarity;
  MOPD remains untested.
- Route passes, locality fails: useful continuation teachers exist but their
  dense parameter update is non-local at this dose.
- Route/locality pass, primary matches shuffled/coarse/off-policy: advantage
  routing is not the causal lever.
- Primary beats controls but not sources/router/sample-more: partial
  integration, not a capability breakthrough.
- Every gate passes: evidence that verifier-backed same-prefix routing installs
  a composable policy beyond the measured single-checkpoint frontier.
