# Adversarial design review

## Verdict

Proceed after the immutable design receipt is committed. The final design
isolates one semantic recovery seam and addresses the duplicate-content,
template-transfer, full-solution-supervision, sentinel, and locality-reuse
failures found during review.

## Objections and resolutions

1. **The first generator only had five variants, so “fresh” blocks repeated.**
   Replaced before preregistration. High-entropy resources/quantities produce
   24/24 unique bank, 24/24 unique calibration, and 32/32 unique dev and
   confirmation contents; bank/calibration and dev/confirmation intersections
   are empty. Tests fingerprint public content while excluding IDs/splits.
2. **Nine renamed copies of one function only test lexical transfer.** Training
   and transfer now span bundle mappings, record dictionaries, and tuple
   sequences with distinct loops and indexing. Each representation has two
   train skins and one held-out skin. The structurally older atomic-reservation
   fixture is separately gated as an adaptive sentinel.
3. **Full seven-row policy blocks would directly reteach whole solutions.** The
   treatment replaces only the 24 post-diagnosis revision rows. The other 312
   rows come from the exact prior bank, including all inspect, ordinary patch,
   rejected-patch, verify, and commit targets. The causal estimand is the
   residual failed-test revision, not a broad policy-task curriculum.
4. **The treatment and control receive unequal dose.** Each has 48 complete
   blocks, 336 rows, identical transition/operator counts, optimizer schedule,
   and exactly matched action mass per transition and operator. Only 24 target
   payloads/prompts differ.
5. **Longer semantic payloads could dominate gradients.** Transition mass is
   calibrated after tokenization; `diagnosis_to_changed_patch` contributes the
   same 12,749.33 weighted action tokens/epoch in both arms. Maximum row length
   is 1,179 versus the 4,096 cap, so no selected row silently drops.
6. **The known atomic failure is not unseen.** Correct. It is named and gated
   only as a sentinel (candidate success ≥.50). All three fresh transfer skins
   must be nonnegative versus parent, and the overall +10/+5/+5 gates cannot be
   claimed as four unseen families.
7. **The partial may still test transaction structure rather than policy.**
   Every policy partial copies the capacity map, rejects unknown/insufficient
   inputs with `False`, validates whole bundles where applicable, commits
   accepted changes atomically, and leaves inputs unchanged. Visible and hidden
   failure comes from the missing negative exception alone.
8. **More attempts might discover the distinction.** Parent sample-more gets
   the same 6,144-token reservation and favorable pass-if-either scoring. The
   candidate must beat it by five points on both transfer seeds.
9. **An extra update from the already useful prior bank could explain gains.**
   The matched `extra_transaction` arm starts from the same exact learned
   parent and trains on all 336 prior rows at the same dose. Candidate must beat
   it on calibration and both transfer blocks.
10. **Success can hide deletion of rare agent pivots.** Every bank retains all
    seven transitions. Gates independently measure rejected→changed patch,
    failed test→revision, verify, commit, invalid actions, answer-cap contacts,
    broad recovery, and normal loops.
11. **Incremental locality against the parent hides accumulated drift.** The
    candidate is compared directly to C54 apex on 48 newly authored contexts.
    Entropy has a floor; varentropy is recorded only as collateral diagnosis.
12. **The copied locality block was not actually fresh.** Every copied stem and
    prefix was replaced. The 48 new content hashes have zero intersection with
    every prior experiment locality file.
13. **Python rendering can change procedural manifests across arms.** Official
    processes freeze the hash seed, content uses no set rendering, cross-seed
    subprocess tests produce the same digest, and analyzers require both
    ID-bearing and content-only manifest hashes to match.
14. **Resuming after a design edit can silently reuse stale artifacts.** GPU and
    full modes require an immutable receipt hashing all design-critical files
    at an ancestor commit. Any frozen-file edit fails closed; the current
    external directory contains deterministic CPU banks only.
15. **Menagerie could leak or be selected posthoc.** Quick 71301 and medium
    71302 are frozen and currently absent from the aggregate-log union. The
    suite stays sealed through locality, controls-first calibration, dev,
    untouched confirmation, and retention. Only public CLI output is read and
    raw files are immediately rewritten to aggregate/per-family fields.

## Residual limitations

- Three representations are stronger than lexical skins but remain one narrow
  semantic rule. A positive supports exception-versus-rejection discrimination,
  not arbitrary validation-policy learning.
- Equal loss mass does not equalize realized gradient norm. Training/merge
  receipts retain loss, step, padding, and delta-norm diagnostics; no posthoc
  rescaling is allowed here.
- Development and confirmation cases are content-unique, but a 64-case
  bootstrap remains modest. Both seeds and every fresh-family direction must
  agree.
- A positive procedural result may not move Menagerie if this policy is not a
  binding blackbox capability.
