## Trigger

The tier forensics closed with CONSTANTS_ARE_INSTRUMENT_ARTIFACTS: the
menders/sirens constants that killed the goal gate at quick/tb1024 are
quick-granularity item-draw artifacts, and the all-ten-families gate passed

passer was a gym-trained arm. The contamination-free universal line (best
quick aggregate 0.5081) has NEVER been measured at medium. The program goal
requires exactly this gate; the venue evidence now says quick was the wrong
courtroom.

## Question

At the medium tier, where do the line's best published composites actually
stand: does the quick aggregate ordering (replay_repeat > designed_fresh >
base) hold, how close is each arm to the recorded goal gate, and which
families block?

## Why believable

Pure measurement of already-published models through the trusted gateway —
no training, one fresh sealed seed, four authenticated explicit merges. The
forensics guarantees strict-win headroom exists on all ten families at
medium (base never ceilings there in 95 historical events). Whatever the
outcome, it either records the program milestone or hands the next dose its
exact design constraints (the blocking families at the right granularity).

## Calibration

A goal-gate PASS by an existing arm is possible but not expected (~10%
historical rate for arms that trained on family data; ours did not).
The high-probability payoff is the intelligence: the medium-tier map of the
line's Pareto set, which every subsequent dose design needs regardless.
