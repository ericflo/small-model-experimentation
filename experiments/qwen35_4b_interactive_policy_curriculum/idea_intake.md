## Novelty Claim

This is the first breadth-oriented update in the repository whose training
distribution is generated by the current policy's live multi-turn state
visitation and whose final objective is the outcome of the whole interactive
trajectory rather than a static target completion.

## Mechanism

DAgger removes covariate shift by teaching the expert correction at states
caused by the model's own earlier decisions. Group-relative terminal-reward
training then distinguishes entire action sequences that cause success from
locally plausible sequences that do not. The explanation is false if matched
additional oracle SFT or shuffled rewards reproduce the gain, if only action
format improves, or if held-out environment families do not move.

## Control Plan

- Baseline: regenerated C53 blend, greedy and matched-compute sampled
  trajectories under the identical vLLM protocol.
- Mechanism-falsifying controls: DAgger-only; matched additional DAgger/SFT;
  reward-shuffled GRPO; constant-reward groups; programmatic oracle ceiling.
- Shift/robustness: environment-family holdout, fresh seeds, harder levels,
  atom/emission retention, natural-close and parse diagnostics.
- Hidden-label boundary: expert actions and environment rewards may supervise
  training, but model inputs contain only visible transcript state. Menagerie
  stays closed until the proxy decision receipt passes and is never used as
  inner-loop feedback.

## Evidence Output

- Program evidence update: whether live-state imitation and/or consequence

- Claim ledger/synthesis: new claim or scoped negative only after controls.
- Reusable artifact: common episode trajectory schema, state-aware expert

- Stop/branch: stop before RL if DAgger cannot learn the visible action

  SFT and shuffled rewards. If RL works only in-family, branch to explicit
  representation transfer rather than spending blackbox seeds.
