Two triggers converged. First, the owner's standalone directive exposed
the winning composite's one provenance boundary: its adapter chain
warm-starts from the undocumented C53-era gym-line `blend` adapter
(~0.44 quick aggregate on its own — load-bearing, and trained in the era

every menders mechanism class closed by rule, the strongest remaining

fresh zero-initialized adapter on the official base, merge, and measure
once at medium beside the original: does the zero-root composite beat
base with a comparable family profile, or is the gym-era prefix

- ZERO_ROOT_DEGRADED: the prefix's contribution at medium is measured

## Calibration

The prefix carried real capability, but five of the six documented
stages are replay/designed doses that repeatedly installed and
compounded on their own instruments; the parent-anchored medium profile
(0.33–0.38 vs base 0.06–0.11) is far above what any single stage
contributed. Prior on COMPARABLE: ~40–55%. Cost: ~2.5–3 h GPU for the
six stage replays + one merge + one three-arm medium event at a fresh
sealed seed. Either verdict is durable knowledge the map needs.
