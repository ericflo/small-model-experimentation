# Adversarial Design Review

**Verdict:** `PASS_SIBLING_COLLECTION`.

This authorizes one sibling collection only after the design commit is pushed and both workflows are green. It does not authorize selection, training, merge, local evaluation, or benchmark access.

## 1. Is residual selection post-hoc?

It is adaptive across experiments, not within this one. The balanced predecessor publicly established the complete per-skill failure distribution and closed. This successor names the deterministic rule—published skills with at least four hard failures—and freezes the resulting ten-skill set before any sibling sample exists. That is the correct result-separated use of prior evidence.

## 2. Does reusing the collection contaminate evaluation?

The inherited tasks are training-source candidates, not held-out evidence. Their executable truth is procedural and benchmark-independent. Fresh local seed 88,012 and the sealed benchmark gateway remain untouched. The successor copies the exact source and inventory it needs and binds their hashes.

## 3. Can oracle truth leak into sibling generation?

No. The 225-row input serializes only IDs, original user messages, and public metadata. Tests reject answer, reference thought, audit, truth, and expected-answer fields. Executable truth is used only after generation to grade samples.

## 4. Are saturated skills being ignored?

They are excluded only from failure treatment. Select, count, and route remain in active replay and in the unchanged fresh all-13-skill local gate. Candidate success requires a strict total win over replay and parent; a residual-only gain that erases mastered behavior cannot promote.

## 5. Is `n=16` an adaptive rescue?

No. The balanced predecessor froze `n=16` but never consumed its sibling seed. This successor uses one fresh seed 66,117, all 225 residual failures, and the same prospectively fixed sampling geometry. There is no second event if one skill misses quota.

## 6. Could shortest correct traces be spurious?

Yes; correctness does not prove faithful prose. Qualification therefore additionally requires natural closure, canonical output, and bounded length, while fresh semantic transfer is the actual test. The causal claim belongs to the complete shortest-success package, not individual rationales.

## 7. Is replay strong enough to falsify the mechanism?

Yes. Replay is an active capability intervention. Both arms will independently continue the same parent and must match exact forward, target, loss-mass, step, and shared-row exposure. Candidate must strictly beat replay. Solver infeasibility stops rather than altering sampled targets.

## 8. Does training evade sample-more?

No claim may. Local and aggregate deploy greedy paired composites, while any eventual universal claim still owes a fresh same-backend matched-compute sample-more comparator. This training collection cannot stand in for that held-out baseline.

## Decision

The residual policy fixes the predecessor’s feasibility error without weakening retention, provenance, compute matching, or held-out standards. Run one sibling event only.
