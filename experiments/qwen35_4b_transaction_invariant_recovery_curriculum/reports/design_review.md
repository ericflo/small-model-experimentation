# Adversarial design review

## Verdict

Proceed. The design tests a missing proposal-level semantic invariant, not
another policy-selection or loop-syntax variant, and its replay-only control
can distinguish the new examples from extra conditional-recovery dose.

## Main objections and resolutions

1. **Programmatic full repairs may simply leak answers.** They are capability
   supervision, deliberately, but only on six procedural training families.
   The claim requires transfer to four distinct APIs and two seeds, plus a
   same-parent replay-only control. Initial/partial/full executable invariants
   are tested for every generated repository.
2. **The transaction arm receives different dose.** Both arms contain 48
   complete task blocks and 336 rows, expose every conditional transition once
   per block, use the same optimizer and epochs, and are calibrated to identical
   action mass within each operator. The only intended difference is replacing
   24 old recovery blocks with 24 transaction blocks.
3. **Semantic action payloads are longer than generic replay payloads.** The
   mass calibration explicitly equalizes weighted action-token loss by
   transition and operator, and the training denominator is identical. Payload
   length is part of the semantic example, not an unmeasured dose advantage.
4. **Starting from recovery-action confounds transaction learning with prior
   recovery training.** That parent is frozen and evaluated directly. Both new
   arms start from its exact hash. The incremental contrast is primary versus
   replay-only; the absolute deployment contrast uses the C54 apex.
5. **The partial patches manufacture the desired recovery path.** Correct: the
   partials create controlled public failure states for retention rows, while
   the full action targets encode the semantic conjunction. The transfer score
   comes from free-running agents on distinct repositories, not teacher-forced
   rows.
6. **`atomic_reservations` is not unseen.** It is intentionally retained as a
   predecessor failure sentinel. Three of four transfer families are new; the
   family gate requires breadth and prevents the sentinel alone from carrying
   the result. Confirmation regenerates every family at an independent seed.
7. **More sampling might discover the full conjunction.** Parent sample-more
   receives exactly the same 6,144-token reservation and the favorable
   pass-if-either hidden score. The candidate must beat it by five points on
   both transfer blocks.
8. **Training may preserve success while deleting loop pivots.** The bank
   includes all seven transitions per task and batches complete transition
   supercycles. Gates separately require rejected-patch recovery,
   failed-test revision, verification, commit, invalid-action, and answer-cap
   retention on older families.
9. **Locality against the parent could hide accumulated drift from the apex.**
   The registered audit compares the candidate directly to C54 apex, making it
   stricter than incremental parent locality. Entropy has a retention floor;
   varentropy is recorded.
10. **Entropy or varentropy should choose where to push.** Prior evidence found
    them useful for routing/acquisition but unsafe as correctness or token-loss
    weights. This intervention chooses examples by a verified semantic failure
    core and uses uncertainty only to detect broad collateral. A later intake
    may route acquisition by uncertainty after this mechanism is measured.
11. **Menagerie could contaminate training or model selection.** It stays sealed
    through locality, calibration, dev, confirm, and broad retention. The suite
    is invoked only via its public CLI with aggregate receipts; no item,
    transcript, result detail, or family source is read.
12. **Python set rendering can randomize otherwise identical prompts.** The
    control-first calibration initially caught this through unequal manifest
    hashes before candidate exposure. Official subprocesses now freeze
    `PYTHONHASHSEED=0`; tests and analyzers require identical manifests. The two
    invalid control payloads are quarantined and regenerated.

## Residual limitations

- Hand-designed transaction families may share recognizable Python idioms;
  transfer across three new skins reduces but cannot eliminate this concern.
- Equal weighted action mass does not equalize realized gradient norm. Training
  receipts and merge-delta norms remain diagnostic; a negative may motivate a
  lower-dose follow-up, not posthoc rescaling here.
- A positive procedural result can still miss Menagerie if transaction
  semantics are not a binding benchmark capability.
