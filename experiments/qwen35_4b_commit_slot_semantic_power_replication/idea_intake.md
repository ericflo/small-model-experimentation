## Mechanism

whether ordered thought contributes semantic information beyond the identical
token bag. A real effect should persist across tasks and aliases, produce mixed
outcomes needed for value fitting, and leave the mask operating on naturally
competitive answers. Failure of task/bootstrap/diversity gates falsifies that
general mechanism at fixed 1,024.
