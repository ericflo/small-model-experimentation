the −5 band while the 80/2-kind arm reproduces its −10 on the same screen. If
both break, the cost is intrinsic to dosing this vehicle. If the −10 fails to
reproduce, the retention screen's seed noise is larger than banded and every
retention reading this session gets re-calibrated.
