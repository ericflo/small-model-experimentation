unique fix from a static trace) and was killed when demonstrated search

line has never trained: the EPISODE PROTOCOL itself. Every row in every
universal curriculum to date is a single-turn atom; both blocking families
are episodes. The candidate curriculum renders act→observe→revise
feedback-loop episodes (the fix is easy; USING the rerun evidence is the
lesson) and hidden-state chain-execution episodes (narrated state tracking
across turns) on invented executable formalisms with fresh vocabulary.

Mechanism precedents, all in-repo: episode-protocol installs are the
line's most reliable wins (hygiene 7/7 all-time, explore, termination);
