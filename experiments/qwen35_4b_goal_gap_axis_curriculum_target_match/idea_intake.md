## Novelty Claim

First trial to attack the benchmark's empirically stuck families directly with
designed, contamination-free, atom-form curricula built only from public axis
descriptions — four content blocks (multi-formalism program repair with enforced
unique fixes; unique-shortest budgeted route search with compact frontier
notation; instruction-hygiene records where obeying an embedded directive yields
a parseable wrong answer; branch-balanced documented-protocol execution) — and
the first whose local gate is prospectively designed to be achievable under the
known induction wall (relative wins and non-inferiority bands, no absolute
per-kind floors).

## Mechanism

Transfer failed for the gym because each axis lived on one surface; the designed
atoms vary formalism and vocabulary within each axis so the circuit binds to
structure. The hygiene block additionally scores injection non-compliance
implicitly by construction (the decoy is the only parseable wrong answer). The
explanation is false if the candidate cannot beat both the parent and the
exactly-matched replay control on held-out fresh axis tasks, or wins there while
regressing the 13-skill retention screen beyond the preregistered bands.
