# Adversarial Design Review

Three adversarial reviewers examined the frozen construction before any GPU
event, focused on the deltas from the proven predecessor pipeline; every
mechanical claim was verified by running code.

## Verdicts by lens

- Truth and shortcuts (axis corpus): PASS. An independent verifier with no
  generator import re-derived all 200 answers (160 training + 40 holdout) from
  prompt text alone — program re-simulation and unique-repair enumeration for
  tracefix, exhaustive walk enumeration validating both route-uniqueness prompt
  claims for explore, value-versus-decoy checks for hygiene, and documented-rule
  re-execution for protocol. Zero wrong answers. No within-kind prompt-side
  shortcut stump reaches 0.8. Every narration fragment machine-matches
  re-execution. The gate input carries no oracle fields.
- Cross-file contracts and lifecycle: PASS. Every receipt pin, promotion-writer
  schema (one shared writer function, unit-tested for parity), stage
  prerequisite, benchmark CLI wiring, seed, and fail-closed TODO-pin was traced
  and byte-verified; all `--check` modes and the full smoke reproduce exit-0.
  The predecessor's merge-pin contract bug is verifiably fixed.
- Promotion statistics: PASS. All ten gate conditions enumerated; none is
  structurally unpassable a priori — the predecessor's induct-floor pathology is
  verifiably absent. The three MAJORs below were wording/reading amendments,
  applied before this freeze.

## Majors, with remediations (all applied)

1. Retention cap/parsed bands versus the parent are tighter than pure replay
   continuation's own measured drift; a band failure could misread training
   drift as content cost. FIX: the preregistration now carries a frozen
   drift-versus-content reading keyed to the replay control's recorded numbers.
2. Same-surface holdout for three of four kinds cannot evidence
   structure-binding. FIX: report/README wording now scopes surface-generality
   claims to the multi-formalism tracefix kind; the prereg carries the
   instrument-scope note.
3. Ties-fail kind wins meet control ceilings: a 10/10 control makes a kind
   undetectable. FIX: the prereg's non-promotion reading now records such kinds
   as "not detectable at this instrument".

## Accepted risks (noted, bounded)

- Tracefix uniqueness is enumerated over the generator's bounded instruction
  grammar; two of forty training narrations overstate uniqueness under an
  unbounded reading. The frozen holdout was verified unambiguous under an
  extended grammar; future regenerations need the extended audit.
- Tracefix formalism counts differ between training (9/13/13/5) and holdout
  (1/3/2/4); the per-kind win is noisier than the row count suggests.
- Explore depth is concentrated at budget 3; the instrument mostly probes
  shallow search.
- The banned-vocabulary audit is token-exact; morphological variants of banned
  attribute words appear as plain verbs ("reaches", "reachable") and are not
  surface leakage.
- The correct-band (−5) sits at the screen's ~1-SD noise floor; band-edge
  outcomes are reported with discordance counts per the prereg.

**Verdict:** `PASS_EXPENSIVE_RUN`.
