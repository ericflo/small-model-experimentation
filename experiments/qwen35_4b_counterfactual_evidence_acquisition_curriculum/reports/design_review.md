# Adversarial design review: counterfactual evidence acquisition

## Verdict

Proceed only through the model-free boundary and immutable design lock. The
design can test installation of a narrow, ambiguity-triggered repository
evidence-search policy. It cannot yet claim a result, general active search, or
a broad capability unlock. Training remains conditional on interface,
lineage-locality, headroom, evidence-utilization, nondiscriminating-search, and exact-bank
gates.

## Objections and mandatory resolutions

1. **The direct predecessor was formally an instrument failure.** Its answer
   cap contacted 12% of turns, so its trajectory contrast cannot itself
   authorize training. This successor uses an outcome-free 1,024/2,048/4,096
   answer ladder, counts *all* length contacts rather than only unusable ones,
   and stops if no rung passes before scored qualification.

2. **The historical 0/54 first-patch result may disappear on the rewritten
   substrate.** Two independent blocks measure the exact start checkpoint
   before training. Low unassisted acquisition and pre-verifier success are
   mandatory; saturation yields `NO_ACQUISITION_HEADROOM`.

3. **The model may be unable to use the evidence even when it is supplied.** A
   fixed host-injected search is a reachability ceiling. It must reach 0.80
   paired success, beat unassisted by 0.30, cover at least three families, and
   pass every channel/query-skin floor in both blocks. It is explicitly
   non-deployable.

4. **Any extra tool result could provide generic thinking time or useful
   context.** A deterministic matched-operator SEARCH receives the same extra
   tool-result slot, and its output is asserted to exclude the designated
   evidence path and marker. Correct injected search must beat it by at least
   0.30 paired success in each qualification block.

5. **This may duplicate `qwen35_4b_early_text_hypothesis_forking`.** That
   near-neighbor externally supplies each bound first-operation hypothesis
   before thinking, performs no training or tool acquisition, and selects on an
   exhaustively searchable two-step DSL. The present endpoint is autonomous
   public-repository search followed by an evidence-faithful first patch after
   weight training. No claim of being the first early counterfactual proposal-
   shaping intervention is allowed.

6. **Earlier active-evidence experiments already improved selection.** They
   selected examples or DSL execution probes from explicit pools and often
   found strong fixed/random baselines or mixed learned-policy gains. This
   design therefore requires a nondiscriminating-search control, actual
   downstream patch correctness, transfer, and matched sampling. Its
   contribution, if any, is
   tool-policy installation rather than the generic value of evidence.

7. **A dyad can differ in hidden shortcuts besides the evidence.** Pair hygiene
   requires identical issue, source, tree/path names, and every non-evidence
   public byte. The pair-static digest replaces only the designated evidence
   file; the literal differing-file set must equal that singleton.

8. **Task IDs or branch labels could leak the answer.** Neither is rendered in
   prompts. Greedy deep evaluation prevents stochastic branch-specific seeds
   from carrying the primary dyad result. Smoke must mutate host IDs/labels
   without changing serialized prompts or the public composed relation.

9. **Two independently valid component mappings can compose into a fixed
   shortcut.** Receipts serialize the end-to-end mapping from task through
   query skin, evidence channel/path and bytes to the oracle patch. Smoke must
   show the composition varies and both policy directions rotate across all
   registered strata.

10. **Fresh IDs do not imply fresh tasks.** Content digests exclude IDs and
    splits. Every block is internally unique, declared block pairs are
    disjoint, and qualification, bank, transfer development, confirmation, and
    retention use separate seed namespaces.

11. **The agent could memorize three evidence paths.** Bank, qualification, and
    transfer use disjoint path-name inventories, with three names per evidence
    channel in each regime. Transfer never sees a bank or qualification path.

12. **The agent could memorize the search query template.** Training and
    qualification cross reference and function-symbol queries; transfer uses
    only a held-out function-signature query. A transfer claim requires success
    under that query shift.

13. **Search may return an answer sentence so this is not semantic inference.**
    Correct: the intended capability is acquiring and binding an available
    public specification, not inferring an absent one. The claim is bounded
    accordingly. Distractor hits and disjoint skins still require locating the
    discriminator and implementing its policy.

14. **A generic “always search” policy could pass inferred tasks.** Explicit
    issues already state the edge behavior. Qualification and later retention
    cap unnecessary pre-patch acquisition at 0.25/0.30, and the equal-dose
    explicit arm tests whether indiscriminate acquisition training explains
    the gain.

15. **Reading the right file does not prove its content controlled the patch.**
    Each correct first patch must pass its own branch and fail its counterpart;
    both branches of the dyad must succeed. An evidence-insensitive compromise
    or one-sided default therefore fails the primary event.

16. **Host-injected evidence contains an oracle search action and plan text.**
    It is used only for qualification reachability, never as the deployable
    primary. Unassisted trained evaluation must generate its own search.

17. **Global operator balance can still erase conditional recovery.** The bank
    balances nine named state-to-action transitions, not only
    INSPECT/PATCH/VERIFY/COMMIT totals. Each transition has 48 rows and exactly
    16,000 weighted answer tokens per epoch; optimizer steps consume complete
    transition supercycles.

18. **The new acquisition rows omit later verification and commit.** Each arm
    includes 24 complete seven-transition prior task blocks. Verification,
    commit, rejected-patch revision, and failed-test diagnosis/revision are
    separately gated on transfer and two older-family retention suites.

19. **Padding rare transition strata duplicates examples.** Duplication is
    deterministic, disclosed, and identical in count across arms. Receipts
    identify padding rows. A positive is still limited to this dose; duplicated
    rows are not described as independent data.

20. **The aligned arm may simply receive more action-token pressure.** All
    three banks have 432 rows, equal transition counts, identical transition
    action mass, zero think loss, the same optimizer and epochs, and matched
    complete-loop replay. Exact training receipts are mandatory.

21. **The explicit arm changes issue content and is not a one-factor control.**
    It is an equal-dose redundant-acquisition control, not the sole causal
    comparator. The within-dyad shuffled arm preserves the primary prompt and
    target multisets and changes only evidence-to-patch alignment.

22. **Training deliberately wrong shuffled patches may cause nonspecific harm.**
    That risk is why the experiment also requires start, explicit-redundant,
    locality, and interface comparisons. Candidate-over-shuffled alone is never
    sufficient evidence.

23. **The shuffled arm could accidentally alter prompt or target frequencies.**
    Targets are exchanged only within each dyad at the binding transition.
    Smoke proves byte-identical messages, exact target-multiset preservation,
    balanced branches, and paired-copy counts.

24. **Plan supervision could recreate the C52/recovery-reason locality failure.**
    Every think token has weight zero. Only the JSON action span receives loss.
    Entropy and varentropy remain diagnostics and never route examples or set
    token pressure.

25. **Hybrid-model padding can change exact logits by 0.3–0.44.** The trainer
    never forwards a padded batch. It preserves logical batch-four dyad and
    supercycle scheduling but slices each row to its real length and executes
    four serial unpadded batch-of-one forwards before summing the numerator.

26. **The chosen parent already differs from the apex.** Start and all arms are
    bound to exact hashes and evaluated directly. More importantly, start is
    audited against apex on the exact new locality contexts before training and
    must itself clear the 0.10/entropy floor. Otherwise the run stops as
    `LINEAGE_LOCALITY_INFEASIBLE` rather than asking an unrelated update to undo
    inherited drift.

27. **Incremental locality can hide accumulated damage.** The mandatory gate is
    candidate directly versus apex, at 0.10 median centered non-target drift
    and entropy delta at least −0.05. Candidate versus start is reported only as
    an incremental diagnostic.

28. **Non-coding locality contexts may miss coding-policy collapse.** They are
    paired with explicit-contract retention, normal coding loops, controlled
    recovery, old broad families, old transaction families, invalid actions,
    answer caps, verification, and commit. Neither locality nor behavior alone
    is sufficient.

29. **The previous answer-cap failure was often valid JSON followed by run-on.**
    Raw output and every cap contact remain in receipts. History is canonically
    reduced to native thinking plus the first valid tool call in every arm, so
    run-on cannot become later-turn instruction while still counting against
    the interface gate.

30. **Canonical history is not the fully natural transcript.** Correct; the
    result is scoped to this fixed harness policy. Raw transcripts permit
    forensics, and no arm receives a different canonicalization rule.

31. **Equal turn reservations are not equal inference compute.** Six ordered
    shallow trajectories are collected for start and incumbent. The comparator
    is the first prefix that overmatches the primary deep case on both actual
    sampled tokens and logical model tokens, including repeated input prefills.
    Prefix membership is cost-only and frozen before outcomes are inspected.

32. **Pass-if-any sample-more uses hidden evaluation.** It is deliberately a
    favorable oracle-coverage baseline, not a deployable selector. The primary
    must beat the stronger of start and incumbent dual-overmatched prefixes by
    five points; full six-trajectory pools remain descriptive oracle ceilings.

33. **The primary could be chosen after seeing controls.** `evidence_binding`
    is fixed before training. Controls run first only to prove gate
    reachability and seal comparator outputs; no control can replace the
    primary or alter its threshold.

34. **Training-family success can hide template memorization.** Calibration is
    only a mechanism gate. The claim requires two wholly held-out families,
    disjoint path skins, a new query skin, development and confirmation seeds,
    per-family nonnegative effects, and channel support.

35. **Two held-out families are too few for “general coding capability.”** The
    allowed white-box conclusion is limited to this procedural family/path/query
    transfer. Broader language requires Menagerie evidence and still cannot
    imply universal agentic capability.

36. **Branches and trajectories are not independent samples.** The
    counterfactual dyad is the primary unit. Development and confirmation are
    separate; paired dyad bootstrap is registered, and blocks are never pooled
    to manufacture a pass.

37. **The agent might solve by searching every file.** Transfer caps mean
    non-source inspections before first patch at 2.0 and requires explicit
    no-search conditionality. Search efficiency is reported by family, channel,
    path regime, and query skin.

38. **Hidden tests run on intermediate patches and could leak through control
    flow or timing.** Host scoring is write-only metadata: it never changes
    messages, generation count, stopping, sample membership, or subsequent
    actions. Tests verify this invariance under hidden-label mutation.

39. **Procedural oracle patches are answer supervision, not self-discovered
    capability.** Correct. They teach a policy on fresh train families. Only
    autonomous held-out execution can support transfer; no claim is made that
    the model discovered the rule without supervision.

40. **Entropy/varentropy may look like convenient selectors after results.**
    Their roles are frozen to post hoc stratification across semantic action
    buckets and quartiles. No checkpoint, row, label, dose, gate, or benchmark
    route may depend on them.

41. **Menagerie can contaminate training or model selection.** It is sealed
    behind qualification, lineage/locality, calibration, development,
    confirmation, explicit conditionality, and legacy retention. Invocation is
    CLI-only and aggregate-only; seed freshness is rechecked immediately before
    use.

42. **A white-box positive can still be benchmark-irrelevant.** That outcome is
    preserved as procedural transfer evidence only. The benchmark strategy gate
    separately requires one tier at +0.02 and neither tier below −0.03 versus
    apex.

43. **A stopped experiment can remain misleadingly “in progress.”** Every
    terminal gate now writes an open terminal-disposition receipt. Lifecycle
    closure is a second, required operation after result documentation is
    committed and pushed; it binds the finished README/report/log, program and
    knowledge surfaces, artifact manifest, status-registry removal, and claim
    decision. A third, non-mutating post-push verification requires that closed
    receipt and regenerated indexes on clean `origin/main` and reruns
    `make check`. The open or unpublished receipt makes an abandoned closeout
    machine-visible.

44. **A reduced lock can freeze only the immediate caller while dependencies
    drift.** One canonical ordered 35-file manifest is shared by the lock
    writer, every model-facing guard, and the trainer. Missing, extra, reordered,
    alternate-path, moving-ref, dirty, or unpushed locks fail before model
    construction.

45. **Unchanged weights do not imply an unchanged checkpoint.** Parent roles
    pin `config.json`, `generation_config.json`, `merge_receipt.json`, weights,
    raw tokenizer files, lineage, and revision. Trained merge receipts bind the
    emitted config and generation config; evaluation accepts only registered
    role paths and rehashes every live surface before loading.

46. **A previously passing smoke can outlive trainer or test changes.** The
    smoke receipt now contains the exact hash of every frozen design input plus
    current bank, encode-only, tokenizer, parent-checkpoint, and context-geometry
    receipts. Lock creation recomputes the full model-free chain and rejects a
    stale PASS.

47. **A hand-written aggregate log can impersonate a benchmark run.** Every
    Menagerie event is paired to a seed reservation written before the first
    public call and completed with the canonical event digest. Resume and final
    analysis independently require exact config/bench/analyzer/lock/auth hashes,
    live full-checkpoint fingerprints, and Git-tree metadata for the public
    instrument; unexpected or duplicate events fail before further exposure.

48. **A forced thinking close is model input, not a separate forward.** The
    stage-two prompt-token count already includes injected close tokens, so
    matched logical compute records them diagnostically but does not add them a
    second time.

49. **A changed patch after rejection need not be a useful recovery.** Transfer
    now gates on a valid changed patch within two turns, preventing an
    `INVALID→PATCH` sequence from satisfying the recovery contract.

50. **A reservation can burn a benchmark seed without an append-only event.**
    Frozen-seed collisions stop before a local call; any local unlogged
    reservation terminalizes as an instrument failure. Completed logged events
    remain analyzer-resumable without another public call.

## Required pre-model boundary

- complete idea intake, preregistration, and this review;
- deterministic CPU smoke and context-geometry receipt;
- bank, shuffle, action-mass, batching, firewall, and sample-prefix tests;
- exact source/start/anchor hashes and external-artifact plan;
- immutable design commit plus digest-locked receipt pushed to `main`; and
- start-to-apex locality-feasibility pass on the exact new contexts before any
  training.

## Residual limitations

- Public evidence contains a concise policy marker. The experiment measures
  acquisition and binding of an available specification, not discovery of an
  implicit or missing contract.
- Every target is a one-file Python repair with one primary semantic fork.
  Multi-file search, long dependency chains, and ambiguous conflicting evidence
  remain untested.
- The evidence inventory has three channels and finite path/query skins.
  Disjoint transfer reduces rote memorization but cannot prove open-world search.
- The primary hidden-test event is an evaluator metric. The model's action is
  deployable, but the system does not observe hidden correctness at run time.
- Locality is measured on 48 next-token contexts and behavior on finite
  procedural families. Passing both is stronger than either alone, not proof
  that all unrelated capabilities are unchanged.
- A Menagerie gain would be evidence on that instrument, not proof that every
  downstream coding harness improves.

No model output or scientific result existed when this review was written.
